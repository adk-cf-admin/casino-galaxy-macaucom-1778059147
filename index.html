About the Casino

Galaxy Macau is our luxury integrated casino resort destination in Cotai, Macau, created with an Asian heart and designed for guests who want gaming, hospitality, dining, shopping, entertainment, and leisure in one connected resort environment. Our destination opened on May 15, 2011, with three hotel brands: Banyan Tree Macau, Hotel Okura Macau, and Galaxy Hotel.

  • Our resort covers over 1.1 million square meters.
  • Our guests can access around 5,000 rooms, suites, and villas.
  • Our Grand Resort Deck covers 75,000 square meters.

Our casino resort expanded with Phase 2, launched on May 27, 2015, bringing Galaxy Macau and Broadway Macau to more than 1.1 million square meters.

This stage added The Ritz-Carlton, Macau, JW Marriott Macau, and Broadway Hotel, strengthening our position as a major integrated resort destination for both leisure travelers and casino guests.Our resort continued to develop with Phase 3, including Raffles at Galaxy Macau, Galaxy International Convention Center, Galaxy Arena, and Andaz Macau. These additions support our broader entertainment, events, lifestyle, and hospitality offering, giving our guests more ways to enjoy Macau through one premium destination. Today, our casino resort combines 8 world-class luxury hotels and offers around 5,000 rooms, suites, and villas. Our guests can enjoy the 75,000-square-meter Grand Resort Deck, which features the 575-meter Skytop Adventure Rapids, the largest Skytop Wave Pool, and a 150-meter white sand beach, along with Galaxy Promenade and a wide selection of dining and shopping experiences.
